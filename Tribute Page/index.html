Patriot Day

A tribute to the tragic event that occurred on September 11th, 2001...

The Twin Towers, NYC, prior to the horrific events

Timeline of the Event

  • 5:45am- Hijackers pass through security screening in Portland, Maine

  • 7:59am- Flight 11 takes off, 5 hijackers are on board

  • 8:15am- Flight 175 takes off, 5 hijackers are on board

  • 8:19am- Flight 11 crew members contact ground personnel about a hijacking underway

  • 8:46am- The hijackers crash Flight 11 into the North Twin Tower

  • 8:50am- North Tower evacuation has begun and the US President is alerted

  • 9:03am- The hijackers crash Flight 175 into the South Twin Tower

  • 9:59am- After burning for 56mins, the South Tower collapses

  • 10:28am- After burning for 102mins, the North Tower collapses

  • 11:02am- NYC Mayor orders the evacuation of Lower Manhattan

  • Early Afternoon First Responders, Search and Rescue teams, and Volunteers continue to converge on the Twin Tower remnants in search of survivors

By the end of the day, the world had changed. The United States had suffered its worst-ever terrorist attack. Nearly 3,000 people were found dead, and the impact of this occurance will alter U.S government, travel, and culture forever.
